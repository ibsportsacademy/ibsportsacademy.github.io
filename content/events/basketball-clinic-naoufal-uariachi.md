### International Expertise Meets Local Talent
IB Sports Academy, in collaboration with **IB Sports Foundation**, was honored to host a high-performance basketball clinic led by **Coach Naoufal Uariachi**. As a **FIBA/WABC Instructor** and the former Head Coach of the **Morocco Men’s National Team**, Coach Uariachi brought a wealth of international strategic experience to our courts.

### Mission: Rise Through Hoops
This clinic was a cornerstone of our **'Rise Through Hoops'** initiative. At IB Sports Foundation, we believe that *Talent is Universal, but Opportunity is Not.* By bringing a FIBA-level strategist to **Don Bosco Ashalayam**, we provided elite exposure to indigenous youth and underserved communities who rarely have access to global mentorship.

### Key Highlights
* **Fundamental Mastery:** Coach Uariachi focused on high-level tactical awareness and refined shooting mechanics.
* **Professional Exposure:** Local coaches and youth had the rare opportunity to learn from a coach who recently led a nation through the **AfroBasket Qualifiers**.
* **Empowerment:** The program was designed to ensure that a child's starting point never defines their finish line, offering structured coaching and safeguarding protocols.

"United we Stand, Divided we Unite again." This event was not just about basketball; it was about building a bridge between global expertise and local potential.
