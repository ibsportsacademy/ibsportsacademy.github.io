### Basketball Without Borders
The love for the game knows no boundaries. On 15th September 2025, **IB Sports Academy** and **IB Sports Foundation** welcomed a very special guest to the capital: **Coach Malkom** from Uganda, Africa. 

A **four-time Division 1 Champion (Uganda & Rwanda)** and a representative of Jerome Randle’s **Unlmted Basketball**, Coach Malkom brought a wealth of international experience to our courts at ITL Public School. This workshop wasn't just about basketball; it was a cultural exchange of passion, discipline, and the global spirit of the sport.



### Elite Training & Discipline
The session focused on pushing our athletes beyond their limits. Under Coach Malkom's expert guidance, players engaged in **Elite Basketball Drills** designed to elevate their game to a professional level.

**Key Training Pillars:**
* **Offensive Precision:** Advanced shooting mechanics and quick-release drills.
* **Elite Footwork:** Championship-level agility used in African pro leagues.
* **Tactical Discipline:** Emphasizing the mental toughness required for high-stakes competition.

### Global Standards, Local Talent
This workshop is a testament to our ongoing commitment to **Global Basketball Collaborations**. By consistently bridging the gap between India and international basketball hubs like Uganda, we provide our youth with the perspective and elite skills needed to compete on a world stage. It’s about ensuring that the training at IB Sports always aligns with the highest international standards.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to Coach Malkom for his passion and to the entire **Unlmted Academy** team for making this "Global Hoops Meet" a resounding success. 

**The grind continues. From Africa to India, we are building the game together.**
