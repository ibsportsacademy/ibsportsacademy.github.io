### The Poetry of Movement
Exercises are like prose, whereas **Aerobics is the poetry of movements**. Once you understand the grammar of aerobics, you can write your own poetry through grace, rhythm, and athleticism.

IB Sports Foundation was proud to conduct the **Aerobics Inter School Championship** on 16th October 2022 at **Delhi International School Edge (DIS Edge)**, Sector-18A, Dwarka. The event served as a platform for young athletes to showcase their discipline and passion for fitness in a competitive yet supportive environment.



### Celebrating Excellence and Fitness
This **Aerobics Inter School Championship** brought together students from various institutions across New Delhi, fostering a spirit of healthy competition and physical well-being. This event aligns with our mission to promote fitness under the *Fit India* and *Khelo India* frameworks, encouraging youth to embrace active lifestyles.

**Key Highlights:**
* **Technical Precision:** Participants were judged on their flexibility, coordination, and rhythmic timing.
* **School Spirit:** A massive thank you to all participating schools for their energy and sportsmanship.
* **Future Champions:** Congratulations to all the winners who demonstrated exceptional "poetry in motion."

We extend our heartfelt gratitude to the management of DIS Edge-18 and all the supporting foundations for making this event a grand success. We wish all participants the very best for their upcoming athletic endeavors!

*Join us as we continue to move, grow, and inspire.*
