### A Cultural Slam Dunk: France 🇫🇷 x India 🇮🇳
On 6th November 2023, the courts at **ITL Public School** witnessed a unique fusion of skills and cultures. IB Sports Academy was proud to host an exclusive clinic led by two distinguished experts from the French professional basketball system: **Guillaume Soares** and **Benjamin Bari**.

Guillaume, hailing from Nice, brings elite experience from the French basketball league as an Assistant Coach at **JAV Basket** (JA Vichy-Clermont) and previously at **SOPCC Basket**. Alongside him, Benjamin Bari shared his deep passion for the game, adding a touch of European "je ne sais quoi" to the training session in Delhi.



### Elite Skill Development
The clinic focused on the "Next Level" of training, moving beyond basic drills to professional technical standards used in the French Pro B leagues.

**Workshop Highlights:**
* **Technical Precision:** Refining shooting mechanics and individual ball-handling under professional scrutiny.
* **Tactical Awareness:** Introducing European-style spacing, movement, and high-IQ playmaking.
* **Pro-Mindset:** Understanding the discipline required to compete in a professional international environment.

### Bridging Borders through Sport
This collaboration was more than just a training day; it was an electrifying global exchange. Having coaches of this caliber, especially from the highly competitive French basketball system, provides our athletes with a broader perspective on the global game.

We thank Coach Guillaume and Coach Benjamin for their incredible energy and for inspiring our players to elevate their game to international standards.

**The journey to excellence continues, one slam dunk at a time.**
