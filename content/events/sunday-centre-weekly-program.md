### Inclusion in Action • Every Sunday
At the **Sunday Centre for Special Children**, we believe every child deserves the chance to play, grow, and shine. This flagship initiative, hosted at **ITL Public School, Dwarka**, creates a safe and structured environment where confidence, independence, and pure joy are built through the power of basketball.

Inclusion isn't just a buzzword for us; it is our core responsibility. Every week, our specialized coaches work with children to ensure that **ability shines brighter than disability.**

### Program Schedule
* **Day:** Every Sunday
* **Time:** 9:00 AM – 10:00 AM
* **Venue:** ITL Public School, Sector-9, Dwarka
* **Focus:** Inclusive Basketball, Adaptive Fitness, and Social Interaction

> **National Recognition:** As featured in the *Times of India*, this initiative at ITL Public School stands as a beacon of social responsibility, fostering a space where differently-abled children celebrate their unique abilities through sport.

### Empowering Through Sports & Inclusion
The Sunday Centre is more than just a training session; it’s a community. Under the **IB Sports Foundation’s** *Rise Through Hoops* initiative, we focus on:

1.  **Confidence Building:** Helping children master new skills at their own pace.
2.  **Social Interaction:** Creating a supportive space for teamwork and friendship.
3.  **Physical Well-being:** Improving motor skills and fitness through adapted basketball drills.

We invite parents and mentors to join us in making a difference. Let’s champion inclusion, talent, and teamwork together, on and off the court.

### Join Us This Sunday
We are always looking to welcome new stars to our court. If you would like to enroll a child or volunteer for the program, please reach out to us.

**Every Sunday | 9:00 AM | ITL Public School, Dwarka**
