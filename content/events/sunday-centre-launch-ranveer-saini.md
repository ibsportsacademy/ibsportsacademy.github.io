### A Champion’s Welcome
The court at ITL Public School witnessed history as **Ranveer Singh Saini**, India’s celebrated Gold Medalist Special Olympian and Para-Golfer, officially inaugurated the **Sunday Centre for Special Children**. 

This initiative by **IB Sports Foundation** is designed to provide a high-quality, inclusive environment for differently-abled children to learn basketball, build confidence, and experience the "Power of Play."



### Breaking Barriers with Ranveer Singh Saini
Ranveer’s journey from being an aspiring athlete to a National Awardee and Special Olympic champion served as the perfect backdrop for our launch. His presence reminded every student and parent that with determination, **ability has no limits**. 

We were also privileged to host his mother, **Mrs. Bakhtawar Saini**, whose dedication to Ranveer’s path mirrors our Foundation’s belief in the power of supportive families.

### Event Highlights
* **The Inaugural Match:** Our young athletes from the Sunday Centre took to the court for a friendly match, cheered on by Ranveer and coached by **Ishwar Bhati**.
* **Special Guest - Vikram Rawat:** We were honored to have Mr. Vikram from **Special Olympics Bharat**, reinforcing our commitment to the global movement of inclusion.
* **Community Spirit:** The students of **Don Bosco Ashalayam** delivered a moving performance, celebrating the unity and diverse talents within our community.

### A New Chapter for IB Sports Foundation
As Coach Ishwar Bhati shared during the ceremony, *"Every dribble and every pass carries the true spirit of resilience."* With Ranveer Singh Saini leading the way as an inspiration, the Sunday Centre is now open to every child who dreams of taking their shot.

**Let’s champion inclusion together, on and off the court.**
